# Limitations

BIVE does not infer guilt, criminality, moral worth, personality, or truth from body language. It produces structured review surfaces.

## Known hard limits

- Nonverbal deception cues are weak and noisy.
- Cultural, clinical, trauma, fatigue, stress, and neurodivergent baselines can alter behavior.
- Compression, lighting, edits, frame rate, and camera angle distort visual features.
- Audio quality, overlap, accent, language, and ASR errors distort voice/text features.
- Public datasets often do not represent war, coercion, corruption, power asymmetry, or local political conditions.
- Models can learn dataset artifacts instead of deception.

## Required output humility

Every production report must include:

- confidence;
- uncertainty;
- counter-evidence;
- missing evidence;
- review questions;
- provenance;
- no automated accusation.
