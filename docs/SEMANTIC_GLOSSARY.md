# Semantic Glossary

- **Behavioral hot spot:** a signal worth reviewing, not proof.
- **Claim tension:** unresolved contradiction candidate between statements or evidence.
- **Evidence event:** atomic feature with provenance, confidence, magnitude, and limitation.
- **Falsifier:** module that searches for reasons the leading hypothesis may be wrong.
- **Manipulation risk:** probability-like review score for pressure, evasion, or verification suppression patterns.
- **Uncertainty:** model humility under weak, missing, noisy, or single-modality evidence.
- **Verification question:** question designed to reduce uncertainty or falsify a hypothesis.
